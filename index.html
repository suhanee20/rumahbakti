History of Rumah Bakti

On August 16, 1966, a group of community activists and senior Selangor State Government officials successfully founded and registered the "Selangor Orphans Education Organization" under Registration Number ARS/Sel/50/66 out of responsibility and compassion for orphans. The Committee has made rapid progress toward achieving the objectives of its formation with the approval of this registration. According to permission document number PHT.G.2/139/75, the Selangor State Government has donated a 4.8-acre plot of land in Mukim Hulu Kelang (HS.231) thanks to the efforts of Y.Bhg Allahyarham Dato 'Haji Harun Idris and his wife Y.Bhg Datin Salmah Suleiman. Y.Bhg Allahyarham Dato 'Haji Harun Idris has been appointed as the Chairman to ensure that this project can be implemented effectively.

In remembrance of the services of the late Dato 'Haji Harun Idris, members of the Selangor Orphans Education Organization have agreed to change the name of Rumah Bakti Hulu Kelang (RBHK) to Rumah Bakti Dato' Harun (RBDH) along with the change of the symbol of Rumah Bakti amended and approved the amendment by the Registrar of Societies on 29 July 2011.

Organization and Administration of RBDH

All policies and management of RBDH are determined by a body known as the Selangor Orphans Education Organization Working Council. Members of the Working Council Committee are appointed from among the individuals who are members of this Organization. Members of the Working Council Committee are appointed from among the members through a General Meeting every 2 years.
